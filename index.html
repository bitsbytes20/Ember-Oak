Our philosophy

Cooking as it should be—honest

Chef Isabelle Vance trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens before returning to Britain to open Ember & Oak. She believes the best food is the simplest food, made extraordinary by the quality of its ingredients and the care of its preparation.

The name says it all: ember, for the ancient warmth of fire-cooked food; oak, for the British countryside where most of our ingredients originate.
